Post Office


A post office, under the Department of Posts, Ministry of Communications, Government of India has been functioning on the college campus for the last few years catering to the communication requirements of the students, teachers and the college office. The facility offers great convenience to the local community as well. The post office provides all the standard services including speed post extended by India Post besides financial services like post office savings, recurring deposits etc which promote habits of saving among the students.


Indian Bank


The AAMEC KOILVENNI branch of INDIAN BANK is located in the THIRUVARUR district at AAMEC KOVILVENNI 614403. The IFSC Code of the branch is IDIB000A153 The working hours of the ANJALAI AMMAL MAHALINGAM ENG COLLEGE KOILVENNI branch of INDIAN BANK are Monday to Saturday from 10am to 4pm* while the 2nd and the 4th Saturdays generally remain non-working days.
